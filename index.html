My Portfolio

Tamil Inscription project

The Tamil Inscription project utilizes a combination of advanced technologies to preserve and analyze ancient inscriptions. Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is employed to digitize and convert inscriptions into editable text, while machine learning and AI enhance the accuracy of identifying script patterns. Database management systems store and organize the data, and 3D scanning is used to capture the physical details of inscriptions for virtual exploration. Geographic Information Systems (GIS) map the locations of these inscriptions, and natural language processing (NLP) aids in translating and analyzing the texts. Additionally, digital archives and web platforms ensure accessibility to researchers and the public, enabling a comprehensive understanding of Tamil heritage
